About The Play It Forward Ensemble

Learn about our journey, our mission, and what drives us to share the joy of music.

Our Story

The Play It Forward Ensemble was founded with the belief that music should be accessible to everyone. We are a passionate group of musicians dedicated to performing for free in various community settings, bringing live musical experiences to those who might not otherwise have the opportunity.

Our commitment extends beyond performance. We aim to inspire, educate, and connect with audiences through our diverse repertoire and engaging presentations. Every performance is a step towards enriching our community through the power of music.

Our Values

  • **Community:** Building connections and fostering a sense of belonging through shared musical experiences.
  • **Accessibility:** Providing high-quality live music free of charge to diverse audiences.
  • **Passion:** Driven by a deep love for music and a desire to share its beauty.
  • **Excellence:** Striving for musical and organizational excellence in all our endeavors.
